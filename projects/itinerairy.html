Why It Exists

I grew up dreaming of distant places — but never had the means to go. Travel felt like a club I wasn’t invited to.

So I taught myself to code, learned to build, and created ItinerAIry — not just an app, but an invitation to explore, wander, and say, “This world is mine too.”

How It Works

Modular Trip Planner

The classic way of using ItinerAIry. Build your itinerary brick-by-brick.

  • Destination & People – lock in where and who.
  • Smart Date Picker – type freely or click “Next Weekend / Next Week / Next Month” for instant suggestions.
  • Budget & Currency – set a ceiling in USD, EUR, VND… whatever fits your wallet.
  • Interest Chips – tap food, culture, beach, hiking, nightlife and the AI weighs them all.
  • Extra Notes – dietary needs, mobility limits, “must-see temples”… drop them here so nothing is forgotten.

Hit “Generate” and watch the engine fuse every brick into a seamless, budget-respecting day-by-day plan.

Free-Form Input Mode (Coming soon)

Type naturally: “A romantic beach week in Greece under $1,200” and watch your dream trip come to life — no forms, no friction.

Wander Mode – “Surprise Me” (coming soon)

Let the AI pick a destination weighted for safety, affordability, and accessibility, with educational context included. Sometimes the best trips begin with “Why not?”

Interactive Map & AI Assistant (coming soon)

Ask follow-up questions like “Make this cheaper” or “I want to sleep in” and your itinerary evolves with you.

PDF Export with Personality

Clean, printable, shareable — ending with the quiet note: “May your days be full of good light and better coffee.”
